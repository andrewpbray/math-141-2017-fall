\textbf{Is Yawning Contagious?} Recall the randomized simulation that we performed in the last class. Our goal was to assess the claim that being in the seeded group was associated, \emph{to a statistically significant} degree, with an increase in the likelihood that a study participant would yawn.

\begin{enumerate}
  \itemsep1in
  \item What was the \emph{null hypothesis} for your simulation?
  \item What was the \emph{alternative hypothesis} for your simulation?
  \item What was the \emph{test statistic}?
  \item Where did the observed test statistic lie in the \emph{null distribution}? 
  \item Was this data consistent or inconsistent the null hypothesis? That is, do you \emph{reject} or \emph{fail to reject} the null hypothesis?
\end{enumerate}
